## What determines the value of a junk car in Calgary?

![What determines the value of a junk car in Calgary?](/images/blog/junk-spare-parts-in-calgary.webp)


Junk car value is calculated, not guessed. Buyers assess several factors before
making an offer.

### Vehicle weight and scrap metal content

Weight forms the foundation of every junk car offer. Heavier vehicles contain
more steel and aluminum. These materials are recycled and reused across Alberta
industries. Because scrap metal markets change, offers can vary from time to
time.

### What vehicle weight means in real terms

A compact sedan typically weighs between 2,500 and 3,200 pounds. A full-size
truck can exceed 5,000 pounds. That difference matters. Trucks contain nearly
double the recyclable steel. SUVs fall somewhere in the middle. Because scrap
buyers pay based on weight, heavier vehicles naturally command stronger
baseline offers. This is why a non-running F-150 often receives more than a
damaged Honda Civic, even if both are equally old.

### Make, model, and production year

Some vehicles retain demand long after they stop running. Popular trucks, vans,
and widely used models often have parts that remain useful. Less common
vehicles usually rely more on raw material value. This is why two non-running
cars can receive very different offers.

### Vehicle condition category

Condition determines how the car is processed.

- Old but complete vehicles often contain working components that can be reused.
- Accident-damaged vehicles may still have intact systems with value.
- Non-running vehicles still contain recyclable materials and parts.

### Salvageable parts that influence offers

Certain components increase overall vehicle worth:

- Catalytic converters
- Engines and transmissions
- Aluminum wheels
- Alternators and starters
- Radiators and AC condensers
- Vehicle batteries

These parts are recycled or refurbished depending on condition.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I sell a junk car without a title in Alberta?**

No. Alberta law requires proof of ownership for any vehicle sale. If you lost
your title, you must request a replacement through a registry office. This
takes a few days but ensures a legal transaction. Buyers who accept vehicles
without titles operate outside regulations.

**Do you buy junk cars in areas outside Calgary?**

**What if my car has been sitting for years?**

batteries dead. That is expected. The core structure and parts still matter.

**How do you determine the final offer amount?**

**Will you still buy my car if it is rusted or badly damaged?**

Yes. Rust affects certain components but not overall scrap value. Accident

metals.
