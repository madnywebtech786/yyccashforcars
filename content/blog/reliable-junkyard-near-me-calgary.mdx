## Understanding different junkyard options in Calgary

Not all junkyards serve the same purpose. Some are self-service yards where
you remove parts yourself. These require time, tools, and favorable weather.
Calgary winters make these visits challenging from November through March when
temperatures drop below minus 20 Celsius.

Other yards offer full service and remove parts for you. These are more
convenient but depend heavily on inventory. Specialty salvage yards focus on
specific makes or classic vehicles. These are helpful for rare parts but not
ideal for unwanted cars. Knowing the type of yard helps avoid mismatched
expectations.

### Types of junkyards in the Calgary area

- **Self-service yards:** You enter the lot near Barlow Trail or 52nd Street SE,

- **Full-service salvage:** Staff locate and remove parts for you in heated

  Fords or vintage trucks. Great for restoration projects in the Foothills
  County area.
